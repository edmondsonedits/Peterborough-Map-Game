How to Play

1. Read the dispatch
Each round begins at your selected station. The timer starts only when you press Start Dispatch.
2. Find the location
Pan and zoom the map until the red centre reticle sits where you think the call is.
3. Confirm your guess
Your time plus any distance penalty becomes your score. Stay inside the target radius to avoid a penalty.
4. Review and reset
See the correct location and your distance, then return to your station for the next call.
