Local experience

Meet Kevin Bernard.

Kevin was born and raised in Northumberland and Peterborough County and has played locally as both a player and goalie. He started Flying V to give skaters dependable, consistent sharpening tailored to the way they skate.

When Kevin cannot be at the rink, his dad Eric helps cover the shop. Kevin's daughter Ella brings a U18 AA hockey player's perspective, while his youngest daughter's competitive figure skating experience helps inform the care given to figure skates.

The goal is simple: your edges should feel right from the first stride.
